International Artist & Muralist Robert Maja .

As an artist, I want to draw images showing the revival of our lives, our experiences and our rural customs. My desire is to communicate the natural shadows of our landscapes and the beauty they convey.

Robert Maja was born in Santo Domingo, Dominican Republic in September 1971 . His talent as an artist was quickly recognized in elementary school and strongly encouraged by teachers and elders.

Cubism

Cubism was an influential art movement pioneered by Pablo Picasso and Georges Braque in the early 20th century. It revolutionized traditional perspectives by breaking down subjects into geometric shapes and depicting them from multiple viewpoints simultaneously.

Cigars

Tobacco, specifically in the context of cigars or "puros," has a rich history and culture. Cigars are typically hand-rolled bundles of dried and fermented tobacco leaves, often associated with luxury and relaxation.

Rooster
